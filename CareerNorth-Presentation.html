The Solution

CareerNorth combines AI-powered job discovery, intelligent matching, and personalized application generation into a single platform.

  • Smart resume parsing & skill extraction
  • Real-time job search across Canadian job boards
  • AI match scoring (skills, experience, salary fit)
  • One-click tailored resume generation
  • Personalized cover letter writing
  • Interview preparation with STAR-format answers
3

Target Users

Who

  • Mid-career professionals (8–18 yrs)
  • Professionals seeking remote/hybrid roles
  • Early-to-mid career advancement seekers

Pain Points

  • 3–4 hour application cycles
  • Applying to 50+ jobs without interviews
  • No visibility into match quality
  • Generic resumes & cover letters

Application Generation
